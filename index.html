Appetizers

  • Caprese Salad

    Fresh tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil drizzled with balsamic glaze.

    • Descriptions

    Caprese Salad** is a classic Italian dish that’s simple, fresh, and full of flavor. It’s made with **ripe tomatoes**, **fresh mozzarella cheese**, and **basil leaves**, typically arranged in alternating slices. The salad is drizzled with **extra virgin olive oil** and sometimes a touch of **balsamic glaze** or **salt and pepper** to enhance the taste. It’s a light and refreshing starter** that beautifully showcases the colors of the Italian flag — red (tomato), white (mozzarella), and green (basil).

    Stuffed Mushrooms are a savory and elegant appetizer packed with rich flavors and irresistible aroma. 🍄🧀 They’re made by filling mushroom caps — usually button or cremini mushrooms — with a tasty mixture of ingredients such as cream cheese, breadcrumbs, garlic, herbs, and grated Parmesan. Main Courses

  • Grilled Salmon

    Salmon fillet seasoned and grilled to perfection, served with lemon butter sauce.

    • Descriptions

    Grilled Salmon is a delicious and nutritious dish celebrated for its tender texture and rich, smoky flavor. 🐟🔥 It features a fresh salmon fillet that’s lightly seasoned with salt, pepper, lemon juice, and herbs like dill or parsley, then grilled to perfection until the outside is slightly crisp and the inside remains moist and flaky. Often served with steamed vegetables, rice, or a fresh salad, it can also be paired with a buttery lemon sauce or garlic herb glaze for extra flavor. The result is a healthy, protein-rich meal that’s both elegant and satisfying — perfectly balancing freshness, simplicity, and taste. 🍋🥗

  • Classic Beef Burger

    Juicy beef patty with cheddar, tomato, lettuce, pickles, and house sauce on a toasted brioche bun. Served with fries.

    • Descriptions

    Classic Beef Burger is an all-time favorite comfort food known for its juicy, flavorful patty and satisfying bite. 🍔🔥 It features a grilled or pan-seared beef patty, seasoned to perfection, and served inside a soft, toasted bun. The burger is typically layered with fresh lettuce, ripe tomato slices, onions, pickles, and melty cheese, then topped with ketchup, mustard, or mayonnaise for added flavor. Some versions include crispy bacon, caramelized onions, or special sauces to enhance the taste. The result is a perfect blend of textures and flavors — juicy meat, fresh veggies, creamy sauces, and a fluffy bun — making the Classic Beef Burger a timeless favorite around the world. 🍟🧀

  • Vegetable Alfredo Pasta

    Fettuccine tossed in creamy Alfredo with roasted squash, cherry tomatoes, spinach, and shaved parmesan.

    • Descriptions

    Vegetable Alfredo Pasta is a creamy and comforting Italian-inspired dish that blends rich flavors with wholesome vegetables. 🍝🥦 It features pasta (usually fettuccine or penne) tossed in a luxurious Alfredo sauce, made from butter, cream, and Parmesan cheese, creating a smooth and velvety texture. A colorful mix of fresh vegetables — such as broccoli, bell peppers, mushrooms, carrots, zucchini, or spinach — is sautéed and folded into the pasta, adding freshness, crunch, and nutrition. The result is a deliciously creamy yet light meal, perfectly balancing the richness of the sauce with the vibrant flavors of the vegetables — a satisfying dish for both pasta and veggie lovers alike. 🧄🧀🌽

Desserts

  • Chocolate Fondue

    Assorted fruits and marshmallows served with rich chocolate fondue.

    • Descriptions

    Chocolate Fondue is a delightful dessert experience that turns chocolate into a warm, shared indulgence. 🍫✨ It consists of melted, silky chocolate served in a fondue pot to keep it warm and smooth. The chocolate can be dark, milk, or white, often enhanced with a splash of cream, vanilla, or liqueur for extra richness. Guests dip a variety of treats — such as fresh fruits (strawberries, bananas, apples), marshmallows, cookies, or pieces of cake — into the melted chocolate using fondue forks. The result is a fun and interactive dessert, perfect for sharing, combining the joy of dipping with the irresistible taste of warm, flowing chocolate. 🍓🍌🩵

  • Strawberry Cheesecake

    Silky cheesecake on a graham cracker crust topped with fresh strawberry compote and whipped cream.

    • Descriptions

    Strawberry Cheesecake is a creamy and indulgent dessert that perfectly combines rich flavors with a fresh fruity touch. 🍓🍰 It features a buttery graham cracker or biscuit crust as the base, topped with a smooth and velvety cheesecake filling made from cream cheese, sugar, eggs, and vanilla. After baking or chilling (depending on the recipe), it’s finished with a sweet strawberry topping — usually made from fresh strawberries and a glossy strawberry sauce or jam. The result is a perfect balance of creamy, crunchy, and fruity textures, with each bite offering a refreshing burst of strawberry flavor against the richness of the cheesecake.

  • Tiramisu

    Classic Italian dessert with espresso-soaked ladyfingers layered with mascarpone cream and cocoa dusting .

    • Descriptions

    Tiramisu is a classic Italian dessert known for its rich and creamy texture and delightful coffee flavor. The name "Tiramisu" means “pick me up” in Italian, referring to the energizing ingredients — espresso and cocoa. It’s made by layering coffee-soaked ladyfinger biscuits (savoiardi) with a smooth mascarpone cheese mixture, which usually includes eggs, sugar, and a touch of liqueur like Marsala or rum. The dessert is then dusted with cocoa powder on top and chilled to let the flavors blend beautifully. The result is a light yet indulgent dessert, balancing the bitterness of coffee and cocoa with the sweetness and creaminess of mascarpone — a perfect harmony in every bite.
